Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Gait Detection application.

What is Gait Detection?

Gait Detection is an innovative application that utilizes machine learning to analyze and assess patient gait patterns, providing physiotherapists with detailed insights and reports.

How do I upload a video for analysis?

To upload a video, log in to your account, navigate to the video upload section, and select the video file from your device. Click on the "Upload" button to start the analysis.

What types of gait metrics can I analyze?

Our application provides comprehensive analysis of various gait metrics including speed, cadence, stride length, posture, and balance.

Can I generate custom reports for my patients?

Yes! After the analysis is complete, you can generate and download personalized reports that include the analysis results and tailored recommendations for your patients.

Is my patient data secure?

Absolutely! We prioritize your privacy and ensure that all patient data is securely stored and protected in compliance with relevant data protection regulations.

How can I get support if I encounter issues?

If you need assistance, please contact our support team via the contact page, and we will be happy to help you resolve any issues you may encounter.
